Western Photosynthesis Conference 2026

January 8-11, 2026 • Los Gatos, CA

Venue and Travel Information

This year's conference will be held at Green Mountain retreat, a venue surrounded by the natural beauty of Los Gatos with easy access to downtown San Jose.

To get to the venue from San Jose International Airport (SJC), simply navigate to “Green Mountain Retreat” in Google Maps. If the GPS navigates to a County Park, continue driving up Bear Creek Road for 3 minutes to reach the venue entrance. A van shuttle will be provided for transport between SJC and Green Mountain Retreat. Please contact the organizers if you're interested.

Green Mountain Retreat also has a number of on-site activities to enjoy, including bocce ball, archery, giant chess, ping pong, pickleball, badminton, axe throwing, Connect 4, cornhole, and basketball.

Note: The weather during the conference will likely range from 43–58 °F, with a slight chance of rain expected at some point during the weekend. Be sure to bring clothes and shoes fit for the cold!
