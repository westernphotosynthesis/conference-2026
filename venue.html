Western Photosynthesis Conference 2026

January 8–11, 2026 • Los Gatos, CA

Venue and Travel Information

This year's conference will be held at Green Mountain Retreat, a venue surrounded by the natural redwood and oak woodlands of Los Gatos with easy access to downtown San Jose.

There are three international airports in the Bay Area that you can plan to fly to or from: San Francisco International Airport (SFO), Oakland International Airport (OAK), and San Jose International Airport (SJC). A van shuttle can be provided for transport between SJC/SFO/OAK and Green Mountain Retreat. Please contact the organizers if you are interested.

To get to the venue from any one of these airports, simply navigate to “Green Mountain Retreat” in Google Maps. If the GPS navigates to a County Park, continue driving up Bear Creek Road for 3 minutes to reach the venue entrance.

Green Mountain Retreat also has a number of on-site activities to enjoy, including bocce ball, archery, giant chess, ping pong, pickleball, badminton, Connect 4, cornhole, and basketball.

A virtual tour of the venue is available at the bottom of this page for your reference.
