January 8–11, 2026 • Los Gatos, CA

Registration

Early registrants will recieve a $50 discount!

Registration costs will cover meals and lodging for all attendees. Complete the registration form here.


Registration Awards

All postdocs, graduate students, and undergraduate students are eligible to receive registration awards that may cover up to two-thirds of the registration fee. To apply, please prepare (1) an abstract of the presentation you intend to submit to the meeting (2) a paragraph explaining how attending the meeting will benefit your career, and (3) a letter of recommendation from your primary advisor, including a statement on available funds. Please send the following materials to westernphotosynthesis@gmail.com by November 7th.

If applying for a registration award, please do not register for the conference until you receive notification of the award decision by November 21st. If your application is successful, we will provide you with a registration code and instructions on how to proceed with registration.


Abstract Submission Guidelines

Please submit a one-page abstract of your presentation in PDF format (8.5 x 11 in. dimensions) with (1) the title of your poster/talk, (2) a complete author list, and (3) author affiliations. Please note that the uploaded PDF will be used directly in our program booklet, so accuracy is crucial.

Feel free to be creative with all other aspects of the presentation and formatting of your abstract!

  • November 25, 2025 Abstract submission to be considered for talk presentation
  • December 19, 2025 General registration and abstract submission for poster presentation
